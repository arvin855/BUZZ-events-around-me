About

“The Buzz Around Me (Event Feeds)” is basically an online social media/e-commers like website that allows one to know/get information about all the events that happen around. One can view all types and category of events that can take place and also buy tickets to attend them as well.



An important characteristic of this application is along with buying of the tickets, a real time google map that allows one to view the location of the events that is going to take place for better accuracy and attractive feature. One can view full details of a particular post and get more information about the event such as location, date, price of tickets and number of available tickets at that point of time.
